Instant noodles

Ingridients

  • 1 pack of instant noodles
  • 2 cups of water
  • seasoning packet(find in noodle packet )
  • optional egg

Instructions

  1. Boil cups of water in a pot
  2. Add instant noodles to pot once water is boiling
  3. Let bnoodles cook for 2-3 minutes
  4. Drain excess water leaving just a bit
  5. Add seasoning packet and stir to combine
  6. optionally crack an egg into the pot and stir till cooked
  7. Serve and enjoy while hot
